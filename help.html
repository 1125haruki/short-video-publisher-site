Help Center

Setup, publishing steps, and support for Short Video Publisher.

This help center explains how creators move from the public website into the publisher workspace, connect TikTok, load creator posting settings, review privacy and disclosure choices, publish through Direct Post, and check publish status from the same product flow.

Getting Started

1

Open the workspace

Move from the public website into the dedicated publisher workspace page.

2

Connect TikTok

Use Login Kit to authorize the creator account and return to the workspace.

3

Review settings and publish

Review privacy, interaction toggles, branded content, and duration limits before Direct Post.

Current TikTok Scope

  • Login Kit for creator account connection
  • `user.info.basic` for the connected account display
  • `video.publish` for Direct Post initialization from the workspace
  • Publish status inspection from the returned publish ID
  • Creator-controlled privacy, interaction, and consent review inside the workspace

Posting Workflow

  • Prepare the public MP4 URL and TikTok title inside the workspace
  • Load creator settings so privacy options and interaction limits come from TikTok, not from defaults
  • Collect creator consent before sending Direct Post
  • Keep the publish ID to inspect status later from the same workspace
